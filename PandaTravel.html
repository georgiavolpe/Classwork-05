Punaluu Black Sand Beach

The Punaluu beach dazzles its visitors with its coal-black sands and exotic flora. As vibrant groves of coconut palms surround the peaceful shore, sea turtles and birds reside nearby. The long stretch of sand serves as a perfect setting for picnics and sunbathing. Whether sunny or cloudy, the Punaluu beach is quite reserved and provides a relaxing and comfortable experience to all who visit.
